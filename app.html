1. Scope of Work

The Contractor, Kevin Quirk, will:

  • Digitise paper-based resident and operational records.
  • Upload records to a secure Microsoft SharePoint environment.
  • Verify completeness and accessibility of uploaded files.
  • Securely shred paper records following written authorisation.
  • Maintain strict confidentiality at all times.

This engagement does not include clinical care, supervision of residents, or operational involvement beyond digitisation.

2. Safeguarding & Resident Contact

The Contractor agrees:

  • All work will be conducted under supervised conditions as arranged by the Care Home.
  • Work will be conducted in designated administrative areas wherever reasonably possible.
  • No direct or unsupervised engagement with residents will occur.
  • The Contractor will not interact with residents during the course of work.
  • The Contractor will not enter resident bedrooms or private care areas unless expressly authorised for access.
  • The Contractor will not provide care, assistance, or supervision to residents.
  • Any safeguarding concerns observed will be immediately reported to the Registered Manager.
  • No photography, recording, or disclosure of resident information outside professional necessity will take place.

Applecroft Care Home confirms appropriate site supervision arrangements are in place and will be maintained throughout the engagement.

3. Data Protection & Confidentiality

The Contractor confirms:

  • All accessed information is confidential and sensitive.
  • Data will only be accessed for the sole purpose of digitisation.
  • No resident data will be retained on personal devices once uploaded.
  • All devices used are encrypted and password protected.
  • Microsoft SharePoint access is secured using Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA).
  • No data will be transferred outside authorised systems.
  • Full compliance with UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

Applecroft Care Home confirms:

  • It holds lawful authority to digitise and destroy records.
  • Retention policies have been reviewed prior to authorising destruction.
  • Written authorisation will be provided before shredding any records.

4. Secure Destruction Procedure

Paper records will only be destroyed:

  • ☐ After written confirmation from authorised Care Home Management
  • ☐ After verification of successful and complete digital storage
  • ☐ Using cross-cut shredding or certified confidential waste disposal

A Record Destruction Log shall include:

  • Description of records
  • Date digitised
  • Date destroyed
  • Authorising manager signature

6. Infection Prevention & Control

The Contractor agrees:

  • Not to attend site if unwell.
  • To follow Applecroft Care Home infection control procedures.
  • To sanitise hands before and after handling documents.
  • To comply with outbreak restrictions or enhanced precautions if applicable.

7. Responsibilities

Contractor Responsibilities:

  • Maintain strict confidentiality.
  • Operate secure digital systems.
  • Ensure safe working practices.
  • Maintain a secure and tidy workspace.
  • Liaise with the designated Archiving Manager regarding records management.
  • Immediately report any data or safety incidents.

Care Home Responsibilities:

  • Provide secure access to relevant records.
  • Provide a designated workspace where possible.
  • Provide a named safeguarding contact.
  • Designate an Archiving Manager as the primary liaison for records management.
  • Ensure appropriate supervision arrangements are in place.
  • Authorise destruction of documents in writing.

8. Limitation of Liability

The Contractor shall not be liable for:

  • Pre-existing data inaccuracies within original records.
  • Retention decisions made by the Care Home.
  • Loss arising from failure of third-party systems outside the Contractor's reasonable control, provided reasonable security measures are in place.

Liability for data handling shall be limited to the extent permitted under applicable law.
