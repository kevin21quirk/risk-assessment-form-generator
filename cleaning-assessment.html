1. Safeguarding & Resident Protection

Understanding of Vulnerable Adults:

Both contractors confirm they understand they will be working in a care home environment with vulnerable adults who may have cognitive impairments, physical disabilities, or other care needs requiring protection and safeguarding.

No Direct Resident Contact:

Contractors acknowledge that their role is strictly limited to cleaning duties and they must NOT engage in:

  • Direct care or assistance to residents
  • Personal conversations or interactions with residents beyond polite greetings
  • Entering resident rooms when residents are present (unless supervised by care staff)
  • Handling resident belongings, medications, or personal items
  • Taking photographs or videos on the premises

Safeguarding Awareness:

Contractors confirm awareness of:

  • Signs of abuse, neglect, or harm to vulnerable adults
  • Duty to report any concerns immediately to the Home Manager
  • Maintaining professional boundaries at all times
  • Respecting resident privacy, dignity, and confidentiality

2. Health & Safety Compliance

Health Declaration:

Both contractors confirm they are in good health and free from any contagious illnesses that could pose a risk to vulnerable residents.

Infection Control:

  • Will follow all infection control procedures including hand hygiene
  • Will use appropriate PPE (gloves, aprons) when required
  • Will report any illness or symptoms before attending work
  • Understand the importance of cleanliness in a care environment

Safe Working Practices:

  • Will use cleaning products and equipment safely
  • Will place warning signs when floors are wet
  • Will store chemicals and equipment securely
  • Will report any hazards or safety concerns immediately

3. Confidentiality & Data Protection

Confidentiality Agreement:

Contractors acknowledge that they may inadvertently see or hear confidential information about residents, their families, or care home operations. They agree to:

  • Maintain strict confidentiality of all information
  • Not discuss residents, staff, or care home matters outside the premises
  • Not share any information on social media or with third parties
  • Comply with UK GDPR and Data Protection Act 2018
  • Report any data breaches or confidentiality concerns immediately

4. Professional Conduct & Behavior

Code of Conduct:

Both contractors agree to:

  • Behave professionally and respectfully at all times
  • Follow all care home policies and procedures
  • Take direction from the Home Manager or designated supervisor
  • Arrive punctually and work diligently
  • Dress appropriately for cleaning duties
  • Not be under the influence of alcohol or drugs while working
  • Not bring unauthorized visitors to the premises

Reporting Obligations:

Contractors must immediately report:

  • Any safeguarding concerns or suspicions of abuse
  • Accidents, incidents, or near misses
  • Damage to property or equipment
  • Any concerns about resident welfare or safety

5. Supervision & Monitoring

Supervision Arrangements:

Contractors acknowledge that:

  • They will work under the supervision of the Home Manager or designated staff
  • Their work will be monitored and quality-checked regularly
  • They must report to the supervisor at the start and end of each shift
  • They must seek guidance if unsure about any aspect of their duties

Access & Movement:

  • Contractors will only access areas designated for cleaning
  • Must sign in/out using the visitor log
  • Must wear identification badges while on premises
  • Must not access locked areas, offices, or medication storage without authorization

6. Contractor Declaration

We, the undersigned contractors, declare that:

  • All information provided in this assessment is true and accurate
  • We have read and understood all sections of this safeguarding assessment
  • We agree to comply with all safeguarding, health & safety, and confidentiality requirements
  • We understand our role is limited to cleaning duties with no direct resident care
  • We will report any concerns or incidents immediately to the Home Manager
  • We understand that breach of these requirements may result in immediate termination
